About Us

Building India's Industrial Communication Backbone

Established in 1977, from its beginnings in Chennai, the company has evolved into a recognised provider of industrial-grade audio communication systems for mission-critical applications.

We specialize in designing and manufacturing Public Address Systems, Paging & Talkback Systems specifically engineered for harsh industrial environments. Our products operate reliably in cement dust, steel mill heat, and chemical plant conditions where standard equipment fails.

IP-Based PA Systems
