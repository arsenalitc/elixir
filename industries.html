Process Industry

Cement Plants

Cement manufacturing environments present unique challenges with high dust levels, extreme temperatures near kilns, and the need for communication across large sprawling facilities. Our systems are specifically engineered to thrive in these conditions.

Key Applications:

  • ✓ Kiln area communication
  • ✓ Raw mill and cement mill zones
  • ✓ Packing plant announcements
  • ✓ Control room to field communication
  • ✓ Emergency evacuation systems

Major Clients:

Dalmia Cement, India Cements, Chettinad Cement, Ramco Cements, Penna Cement, and more.

Power Plants

Power generation facilities require reliable communication for safe operations, especially during emergencies. Our systems provide clear audio in high-noise turbine halls and outdoor switchyards.

Key Applications:

  • ✓ Boiler area paging
  • ✓ Switchyard communication
  • ✓ Control room to field units
  • ✓ Emergency shutdown coordination

NLC India, TANGEDCO, NTPC, TNEB, and various thermal power stations.
