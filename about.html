Our Story

Who We Are

Building India's Industrial Communication Backbone

Elixir Electronics was established in 1977 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with a vision to provide reliable, industrial-grade communication solutions to India's growing manufacturing sector. From its early beginnings, the company has grown into one of India's most trusted manufacturers of industrial PA and talkback systems.

For over 47 years, we have been at the forefront of industrial audio communication technology. Our journey began during India's industrial expansion era, and we have grown alongside the nation's largest cement plants, steel mills, power stations, and refineries.

Today, our systems operate in some of the most demanding environments across India and beyond - from the dusty kilns of cement plants to the extreme heat of steel blast furnaces, from the critical zones of nuclear facilities to the precision-demanding environments of space research centers.

Our Journey

Milestones in Excellence

Key moments that have shaped our legacy in industrial communication.

1977

Foundation

Elixir Electronics established in Chennai, beginning our journey in industrial audio communication.

1985

First Major Contract

Secured first major contract with a leading cement manufacturer, establishing our presence in process industries.

1995

Government Partnerships

Began supplying to Indian Railways, BHEL, and Department of Atomic Energy.

ISO Certification
